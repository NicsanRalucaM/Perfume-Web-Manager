Splendid Tubereuse Mystique
by Bvlgari



TWanting to highlight the potency of this flower, perfumer Sophie Labbé imagined for this creation a twilight tuberosis in the dark. He made a combination of vanilla absolute and davana essence with amber shades of myrrh. The perfume is a powdery oriental-floral, elegantly sweet and ideal for the evening.


Libre
by Yves Saint Laurent



Freedom comes in the form of an iconic perfume. A new floral fragrance, cool and sexy. The lascivious, masculine notes of lavender meet the burning femininity of the orange blossom, merging. A perfume with ingredients harvested both in France and in Morocco. A perfume designed in both Paris and New York. An androgynous fragrance, between feminine and masculine, is like a reinvention of the emblematic feminine tuxedo Yves Saint Laurent.

Stellar Dreamers
by Ex Nihilo



The new fragrance exclusive says that it dedicates this new hypnotic creation to visionaries and dreamers. It is inspired by the latest conquest of space, thus depicting new frontiers and adventures.
